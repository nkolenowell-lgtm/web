About Poetry ZM

Poetry ZM is Zambia's premier online platform dedicated to celebrating, guading school leaver about how to apply to Zambian's Tertiary Institutions and promoting Zambian poetry. We showcase the voices of talented poets from across the nation, providing a space where words come alive and emotions find their rhythm.

Founded by a passionate student at the University of Zambia, Poetry ZM aims to preserve and elevate Zambian literary culture in the digital age.
